INSPIRATION

Having a world that floats amongst the clouds allows for its own unique challenges but allows for the imagination to run wild of the wildlife that would inhabit such a planet.


Movies such as Treasure Planet and avatar where a huge inspiration to the forms of transport and wildlife that the world would have and Avatar is the inspiration for the islands themselves.
